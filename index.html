Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ADWStudio ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the GeoSignal mobile application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device information: We collect information about your mobile device, including device type, operating system version, and a unique device identifier. This is used solely to deliver push notifications.
  • Usage data: We collect information about how you use the App, such as which features you use and how often you scan for signals. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Purchase information: If you subscribe to GeoSignal Pro, your payment is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your credit card details. Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat.
  • Notification preferences: Your notification threshold and signal type preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver push notifications when investment signals are detected
  • Process and manage your Pro subscription
  • Improve the App and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API: We send news queries to Anthropic's API to generate investment signals. No personal user data is included in these queries. Anthropic's privacy policy applies: anthropic.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat's privacy policy applies: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Expo / EAS: Used to deliver the App and push notifications. Expo's privacy policy applies: expo.dev/privacy

4. Data Storage

Most data in GeoSignal is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. This includes your signal history, watchlist, and notification preferences. We do not operate a central database of user data.

5. GDPR — Your Rights (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: You can request that we delete your data.
  • Right to restriction: You can request that we restrict processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to our processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at adwstudio.info@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the App's services. Data stored locally on your device is retained until you uninstall the App or clear the App's data. Subscription data managed by RevenueCat is retained according to their retention policy.

7. Children's Privacy

GeoSignal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications when investment signals are detected. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or within the App under the Alerts tab.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
